Our Mission Statement

Established in 2019, Kosmos is dedicated to spreading K-Pop's phenomenal influence and the excellence of Korean culture around the world.

2019년부터 코스모스는 K-Pop과 한국 문화를 전세계적으로 알리기 위해 활동하고 있습니다. 다양한 커버들로 대중을 사로잡으려고 합니다. 멋진 퍼포먼스를 많이 보여드릴 예정이니, 꼭 지켜봐주세요!



History of KOSMOS @ Cal

KOSMOS was founded in Fall 2019 by UC Berkeley students Vivian Le and Sean Kelly. They wanted to create a cover Twice's "Fancy" with a group of friends, which slowly turned into the creation of more and more kpop covers of popular artists like BTS, Itzy, TXT, and Blackpink. Following the remote semesters of Fall '20 and Spring '21, KOSMOS has grown massively into a full-fledged dance team, producing 10+ dance covers per semester and now performs semesterly at events like the EAU Night Market and AFX Showcase.
